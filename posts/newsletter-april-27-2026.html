✈️🇨🇦 Flight Deals from $164 + 10,000 Bonus Flying Blue Miles + New Loyalty Program Partnership!

Hey Travellers! 

Looking to book an international flight and want to sweeten the deal?

Until May 3rd, 2026, you can earn a bonus of 10,000 Flying Blue Miles on top of the regular miles you’ll already earn when you book an Economy class itinerary from Canada to anywhere Air France or KLM fly. Just make sure you have a valid Flying Blue account when booking your trip.

Flying Blue Miles are typically valued at 1.5 - 2.0 CAD cents each, which means this bonus provides an extra $150 - $200 CAD in value. Not bad!

For more information on this promo, click here.

On another note, if you collect WestJet Rewards Points and Petro-Canada Points, you’ll want to read about this news! These two loyalty programs will form a partnership beginning sometime in early 2027, providing benefits such as earning 20% more Petro-Points on every purchase, the ability to convert Petro-Points to WestJet Points, and much more. 

For more information on this exciting new partnership coming down the road, read my latest blog about it here!
